The Best Movies According To Joshua

My top 3 movie series of all time

Game of thrones

This movie series is so great it keeps you glued to your screen. Its wonderful plot and acting is unrivalled.

Breaking bad

This movie depicts human nature in the sense that a good person may turn out to be everything he ever stood against when circumstances beyond your control come into play.

Bob hearts Abishola

A comedy series set around a Nigerian immigrant and how they navigate through work and relationship in the US
